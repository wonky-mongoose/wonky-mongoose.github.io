Introduction

Memowise offers categorized decks of cards on programming related concepts. Each card has a question which you answer in your head. When you ready to check the answer, you will be given the option to rate yourself. Based on the rating you give yourself, the probability of the card appearing again will increase or decrease. This is a simple but honest way to learn something.
